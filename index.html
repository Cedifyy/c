My Dream Destinations

Tokyo, Japan

As a person who watches anime, I think it's pretty obvious that one of my dream destinations is Japan. Tokyo, the capital is a place of technology and anime. Aside from being an anime fan, I'm also interested in famous places like the Shibuya Crossing and Akihabara.

Kyoto, Japan

While Tokyo is the place of the technology and anime, Kyoto is where the beautiful Japanese shrines and gardens are. In short, it is the place of Japanese culture. It is where most of the famous Japanese shrines are; and it's Japan, who would say no to Japanese delicacies like sushi, ramen, and omurice.

San Francisco, USA

Besides being interested in Japanese culture, I am also a fan of basketball. I watch the NBA regularly and my favorite team is the Golden State Warriors. I've been a fan of this team since 2014. San Francisco is the home of the Warriors. It also has beautiful structures like the Golden Gate Bridge.

Santorini, Greece

Just like what I've mentioned above, I'm a person who is easily amazed of famous and beautiful places. Santorini is a place that you'd think of in your dreams. The coastal view, the eye-catching white structures, and just imagine the beautiful sunset before dusk.

Paris, France

Paris, the city of love. Even if you go alone, you'd still fall inlove especially with the surroundings. The famous Eiffel Tower, the lights at night, the buildings around, and the culinary delights. Imagine relaxing with your significant other during sunset with the view of the Eiffel Tower, drinking hot espresso, and snacking on famous croissants and baguettes.
